ParkPeer users can permanently delete their account and associated personal data either inside the application or by submitting an external request.

Delete inside ParkPeer

  1. Open ParkPeer.
  2. Open Profile.
  3. Select Account security.
  4. Select Permanently delete ParkPeer account.
  5. Confirm the Google account linked to the ParkPeer profile.
  6. Review the final warning and confirm deletion.

Deletion cannot be completed while an active parking exchange is in progress. Complete or safely cancel the active exchange first, then repeat the steps.

Important for Pro users: deleting the account or uninstalling ParkPeer does not automatically cancel an active Google Play subscription. Cancel it separately through Google Play → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → ParkPeer.

Request deletion without the application

If you no longer have ParkPeer, changed devices, lost access, or cannot complete deletion inside the application, send an email to:

ParkPer.dev@gmail.com

Use the subject:

ParkPeer account deletion request

Include:

  • the telephone number associated with the ParkPeer account;
  • your ParkPeer display name, if known;
  • a short explanation that you want the account and associated data permanently deleted.

Do not send your SMS verification code, Google password, device password, or any other password by email. We may request an additional safe verification step to confirm that the account belongs to you.

Data deleted

After a valid deletion request is completed, ParkPeer deletes the active account and associated data, including:

  • Firebase Authentication account;
  • ParkPeer profile and verified telephone number;
  • linked access information;
  • vehicles and active-vehicle selection;
  • notifications and messaging token;
  • P balance records and wallet transactions associated with the active profile;
  • Trial/Pro entitlement associated with the active profile;
  • active parking publications and reservations;
  • Smart sessions and Smart matches;
  • reservation blocks and other active account subcollections.

Data that may be retained without personal identity

Completed parking, trust, safety, and system events may be retained only after account identifiers and private vehicle details are removed or replaced with a non-identifying deleted-account value. This limited information may be used for:

  • fraud and abuse prevention;
  • safety and dispute investigation;
  • aggregated service statistics;
  • parking prediction and application-quality improvement;
  • compliance with applicable law.

Privacy-preserving parking prediction events that were created with an anonymous installation identifier may be retained for up to 365 days.

Google Play may retain purchase, payment, tax, refund, and subscription records under its own obligations. ParkPeer may retain limited non-card transaction references where required for verification, refunds, fraud prevention, accounting, or legal compliance.

Processing time

We will acknowledge and complete a valid request within a reasonably necessary period after verifying account ownership. Additional time may be required for an active security investigation, legal obligation, or unresolved parking exchange. We will explain any applicable delay or legally required retention.
